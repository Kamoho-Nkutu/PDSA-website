Two-Factor Authentication

Add an extra layer of security to your PDSA Veterinary Clinic account by enabling Two-Factor Authentication (2FA). When enabled, you'll need to provide a verification code in addition to your password when signing in.

Currently inactive

Why enable Two-Factor Authentication?

  • Enhanced Security: Protects your account even if your password is compromised
  • Protect Personal Information: Keeps your pet's medical records and personal data secure
  • Prevent Unauthorized Access: Ensures only you can access your account, even if someone else knows your password
  • Secure Transactions: Adds an extra layer of protection for any payments or prescription requests

Set Up Two-Factor Authentication

Choose your preferred method for receiving verification codes:

SMS Text Message

Receive verification codes via text message to your mobile phone. This is the easiest method to set up and use.

Authenticator App

Use an authenticator app like Google Authenticator or Authy to generate verification codes. Works even without mobile reception.

Email Verification

Receive verification codes via email. This method is convenient if you always have access to your email account.

Tips for Success

• Keep your phone secure and with you when accessing your account

• Save backup codes in a secure location

• Update your verification methods if you change your phone number
