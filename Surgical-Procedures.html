Surgical Procedures

Our veterinary surgical team provides compassionate and expert care for a wide range of surgical procedures. From routine spays and neuters to complex orthopedic surgeries, we utilize the latest techniques and technology to ensure the best outcomes for your pets.

Common Surgical Procedures

We perform a wide range of surgical procedures to address various health conditions in pets:

Spay & Neuter

Routine sterilization procedures to prevent unwanted litters and provide health benefits.

Orthopedic Surgery

Fracture repairs, cruciate ligament repairs, and other bone and joint procedures.

Soft Tissue Surgery

Mass removals, abdominal surgeries, and other non-orthopedic procedures.

Dental Surgery

Tooth extractions, oral tumor removals, and other dental procedures.

Ophthalmic Surgery

Eyelid corrections, cataract removal, and other eye procedures.

Emergency Surgery

Foreign body removal, wound repairs, and other urgent procedures.

Post-Surgical Recovery

Proper aftercare is essential for your pet's recovery. Our team will provide detailed instructions tailored to your pet's specific procedure.

Home Care

Instructions for confinement, activity restriction, and monitoring your pet's incision site.

Medication

Pain management and antibiotics as needed to keep your pet comfortable and prevent infection.

Nutrition

Dietary recommendations to support healing and recovery.

Follow-up

Scheduled recheck appointments to monitor healing and remove stitches if necessary.

When to Call the Vet

Contact us immediately if you notice any of the following during your pet's recovery:

  • Excessive swelling, redness, or discharge from the incision site
  • Loss of appetite for more than 24 hours
  • Lethargy or depression lasting more than a day
  • Difficulty breathing
  • Vomiting or diarrhea
  • Signs of pain that aren't controlled with medication
